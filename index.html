About Me

Hi there, my name is Alejandro. I'm a passionate artist and photographer that loves to capture the beauty of the world I live in. I want to use my camera and artwork to convey my own viewpoint with the world, arouse feelings, and tell tales. As an artist, I get inspiration from both the commonplace and remarkable scenes that I see around me. I'm always looking for beauty in all its forms, whether it's in the way light and shadow interact, the vivid hues of nature, or the unadulterated emotion depicted in a photograph.
